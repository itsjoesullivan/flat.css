Headings are in Futura, conveying a sense of modernity and permanence.

Paragraphs

Spacing between lines is exaggerated. Visitors are encouraged to read between them.

When Arthur Manchek stepped into the small soundproofed booth and sat down before the telephone, he knew exactly what he was going to do--but he was not very sure why he was doing it.

Italics

Italic type is slanted to the right, creating the illusion of cursive.

"Beauty is truth, truth beauty," - that is all Ye know on earth, and all ye need to know.
